Sample #26 for yaneSDK2nd

A simple sample of CFastDraw and CFastPlane.

Supports high-speed constant saturated addition, constant saturated subtraction,
constant blending, display in 256-color mode, and more.

I plan to make this class the foundation of drawing classes in the future.
